Welcome!

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands. It was started by a couple who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourists visiting the area. Experience Te Ao Māori with the use of karakia for blessing food and other Māori culture elements.
